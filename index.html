Overview

Welcome to the Plagiarism Awareness website, designed to educate individuals about the importance of originality in writing and the serious consequences of plagiarism. Our goal is to provide resources, examples, and insights to help you avoid unintentional plagiarism.

What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is the act of using someone else's work, ideas, or expressions without proper acknowledgment. It is a significant ethical issue in academia and beyond, undermining the integrity of creative and scholarly work. Understanding plagiarism and how to avoid it is crucial for students, educators, and professionals alike.
